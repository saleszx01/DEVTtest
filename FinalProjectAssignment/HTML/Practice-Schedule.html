Practice Tips

  • Practice regularly, even if only for 10 minutes a day
  • Stay relaxed and comfortable
  • Focus on accuracy, not speed
  • Steps to improve your practice:
    • Warm up fingers gently
    • Practice chord changes slowly
    • Use a metronome to keep time
    • Record your practice to track progress
  • Important reminders:
    • Take breaks to avoid strain
    • Enjoy the process
