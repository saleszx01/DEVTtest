Weekly Beginner Practice Schedule

Here's a suggested weekly plan to help beginners build muscle memory, rhythm, and confidence.

Beginner Guitar Weekly Practice Schedule
Day Warm-up (min) Chord Practice Song Practice Notes
Monday 10 C and G major Twinkle Twinkle Focus on switching between chords
Tuesday 10 Em and D Row Row Row Your Boat Keep fingers relaxed
Wednesday 5 Review all chords Short riffs Optional light day
Thursday 10 C, D, and G Three Little Birds Try playing along with a metronome
Friday 15 Switching exercises Any favorite song End week strong!
